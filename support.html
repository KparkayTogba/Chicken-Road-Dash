Chicken Road Dash

Support & Help

How to Play

Swipe on screen, use the D-pad buttons, or press arrow keys (desktop) to move your chicken. Reach the Finish row at the top to score points. Dodge cars — you have 3 lives. Each successful crossing makes traffic faster.

FAQ

The game doesn't respond to my swipes.

Make sure you're swiping on the game board itself, not outside it. Short, quick swipes work best. You can also use the D-pad buttons below the board.

There's no sound.

Tap the speaker icon in the top-right corner of the sidebar to toggle sound on. Make sure your device is not on silent mode.

My best score was reset.

Best score is saved to your device's local storage. Clearing app data or reinstalling the app will reset it.

The app crashes or won't open.

Try closing the app fully and reopening it. If the problem persists, delete and reinstall the app, then contact us below.

How do I restart mid-game?

Tap the Restart button in the sidebar at any time, or press R on a keyboard.
